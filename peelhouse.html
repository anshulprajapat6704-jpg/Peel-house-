/ About

Stickers, made by hand, for everything you own.

Peelhouse started as a one-person project: a few designs, a sheet of vinyl, and a laptop that needed decorating. It's still small — every order is packed by hand — but the sheet keeps growing.

We design across four lanes: cute characters, funny one-liners, aesthetic minimal marks, and nature-inspired vibes. If it fits on a laptop lid or a water bottle, it's fair game.

What we use

Every sticker is printed on waterproof, UV-resistant vinyl that holds up to bottles, rain, sun, and the inside of a backpack. Matte, glossy or holographic finishes depending on the design.

Where we ship from

Chandigarh, India. Most orders go out within 2–4 business days.

Got a design idea?

We take suggestions and custom requests. and tell us what you want to see on the sheet.

/ Shipping

Shipping

Processing time

Orders are packed by hand and usually leave within 2–4 business days of being placed. During sales or new drops this can stretch to 5–7 days — we'll always let you know if there's a delay.

Delivery time

  • Metro cities: 3–5 business days after dispatch
  • Rest of India: 5–8 business days after dispatch

Shipping cost

A flat ₹40 covers any order size.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll get a tracking link by email or WhatsApp.

Where we deliver

Currently shipping within India only.

/ Returns

Returns & refunds

Damaged or wrong items

Stickers are made-to-order in small batches, so we don't accept returns for "changed my mind." But if your order arrives damaged, or you received the wrong design, message us within 7 days of delivery with a photo and we'll send a replacement or refund — your choice.

Lost in transit

If tracking shows no movement for over 10 days, contact us and we'll follow up with the courier or send a replacement.

How refunds work

Approved refunds are sent back to your original payment method and usually show up within 5–7 business days.

Cancellations

You can cancel for a full refund as long as the order hasn't shipped yet. Message us as soon as possible.

/ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are the stickers made of?

Waterproof vinyl with a UV-resistant laminate, so they hold up to sun, rain, and dishwashers (bottle stickers, anyway).

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 2–4 business days, then 3–8 business days in transit depending on your location.

Do you ship internationally?

Not yet — India only for now.

Can I request a custom design?

Yes — with your idea and we'll let you know if we can make it happen.

What if my stickers arrive damaged?

Send us a photo within 7 days of delivery and we'll replace or refund them.

How do I pay?

Card, UPI, or netbanking through our secure checkout. You can also choose pay-on-confirmation at checkout.
